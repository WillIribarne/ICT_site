Act for Our Planet!

Introduction:

Welcome, Climate Champion. Today you join an elite squad charged with gathering critical climate data, crafting compelling evidence, and launching a targeted awareness campaign. You’ll engage with authentic resources, harness interactive authoring tools, and collaborate with fellow champions.

Initially, we must prepare ourselves and scout the current climate state of the world. We’ll then use the data to craft compelling evidence that will be shared with the world. Finally, we’ll launch our targeted awareness campaign, which will engage with the audience and inspire them to take action.

Are you willing to do what it takes to save the world?.


Task:

List of missions:

1. Gather data on the current climate state of the world.

Instructions: compile one key statistic and one piece of visual evidence (image or graph) from each of our seven curated resources. The list of our seven curated resources can be found here.

2. Use the data you gathered and create an eye-catching infographic using Canva.

Instructions: Open the Canva template, add your title, chart, summary (2–3 sentences), and call-to-action. Share on the dashboard, collect three peer comments.

3. Reinforce core climate vocabulary through an interactive Wordwall activity.

Instructions: Use Wordwall to build a five-question quiz on our unit’s key terms. Embed or link the finished activity on the dashboard and invite classmates to complete it.
